About JobTalk

JobTalk is a cloud-based cross-platform mobile application designed for companies and startups that evaluates candidates’ performance in various fields, such as sentiment analysis, language proficiency, self-expression, and communication skills. The application ensures a fair evaluation process for all candidates by eliminating human bias through the machine learning model trained to assess candidate skills regardless of appearance, background, or ethnicity.
